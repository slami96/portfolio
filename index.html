About Me

Multimedia Designer & Creative Developer

Personal

I'm Adam Slamen, a 2nd year AP Degree in Multimedia Design student at Business Academy Aarhus, based in the vibrant city of Aarhus, Denmark. I'm passionate about creating digital experiences that blend creativity with functionality.

Design Philosophy

"I believe great web design should tell a story and evoke emotions."

My approach focuses on user-centric design with attention to micro-interactions, inspired by Nordic minimalism mixed with bold, playful elements.

Skills & Tools

Technical

Design

Specialization: Interactive 3D web experiences and smooth animations

Background & Interests

I started coding during high school and fell in love with the creative possibilities of web development. My work is inspired by Danish design heritage and modern digital art.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy mountain sports and photography, which greatly influence my dynamic design approach. My dream is to work for a creative agency or start my own studio in the future.
